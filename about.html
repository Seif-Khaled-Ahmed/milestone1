About Us

Our History

Porsche is a German automobile manufacturer specializing in high-performance sports cars, SUVs, and sedans. Founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche, the company initially offered consulting and vehicle development services.

In 1948, Porsche produced its first car, the iconic Porsche 356, which set the stage for the brand's success in motorsports and the production of legendary sports cars.

Our Mission

At Porsche, our mission is to create exceptional driving experiences that embody the perfect balance of performance, design, and innovation. We strive to push the boundaries of automotive engineering while maintaining the heritage and tradition that define the Porsche brand.

Notable Achievements

  • Record 19 overall victories at the 24 Hours of Le Mans
  • Development of the iconic Porsche 911, one of the most recognizable sports cars in history
  • Introduction of the Porsche Taycan, the brand's first all-electric sports car
